ABOUT US

A Vietnamese diner located in London's Covent Garden, Com Viet prides itself in the quality and freshness of its authentically prepared cuisine. Freshly prepared every day, using high quality ingredients selected for their authenticity, Com Viet’s cuisine is entirely taken from recipes passed down through the generations and given new life by the restaurant’s experienced chefs.

Our menu reflects the best of the country’s age-old recipes. Taste flavours inspired by Hanoi home cooking, refined imperial Hue cuisine and dishes that combine Southeast Asian and French colonial-era elements .

We believe in relaxed vibrant dining at Com-Viet, and we serve up some of Vietnam’s tasty street foods and snacks that are great fun to share.
